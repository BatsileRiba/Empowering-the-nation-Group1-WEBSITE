About Us

Founded in 2018 by Precious Radebe, empowering the nation is an initiative that equips gardeners and domestic workers with skills needed to advance in their careers. Precious’ parents and other elderly relatives were never given the chance to equip themselves, so this is her way of supporting similarly affected members from her community. So far, hundreds of gardeners and domestic workers have obtained these skills with Precious’ help, along with her tutors
